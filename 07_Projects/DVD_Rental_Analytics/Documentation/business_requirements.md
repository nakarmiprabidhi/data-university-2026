# Business Requirements

## Project Stakeholders

- Chief Executive Officer
- Finance Manager
- Marketing Manager
- Operations Manager
- Store Managers

## Executive Dashboard Requirements

The leadership team needs the following KPIs:

1. Total number of customers
2. Active and inactive customer counts
3. Customers per store
4. Total number of rentals
5. Average configured rental duration
6. Average actual rental length
7. Total revenue
8. Revenue by store
9. Most rented films
10. Most profitable categories

## Business Questions

### Customer Analysis

- How many customers are currently active?
- How are customers distributed between stores?
- Which customers rent most frequently?
- Which customers generate the most revenue?

### Rental Analysis

- How many rentals have been completed?
- How long do customers keep movies?
- Which films are rented most often?
- Which categories are most popular?

### Financial Analysis

- How much total revenue has been generated?
- Which store generates the most revenue?
- Which customers generate the most revenue?
- How does revenue change over time?
